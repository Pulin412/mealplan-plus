# Getting Started — iOS

## What is MealPlan+?

MealPlan+ is a meal planning and nutrition tracking app designed for people managing diabetes or following specific dietary plans. It lets you build a personal food database, create reusable meal and diet templates, plan your week, log what you eat, and track health metrics.

---

## Installation

Currently available as a direct install (TestFlight or direct IPA). App Store listing is planned.

1. Accept the TestFlight invitation link shared with you.
2. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store if you haven't already.
3. Open the invitation link — tap **Install**.

---

## Creating an Account

### Sign in with Google
Tap **Continue with Google** and select your Google account. No password needed.

### Email & Password
1. Tap **Create an account**.
2. Enter your name, email, and a password (minimum 6 characters).
3. Tap **Sign Up**.

> Forgot your password? Tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen. You'll receive a Firebase reset email.

---

## First Launch

After signing in, the app seeds your account with starter foods, meals, and diet templates to get you up and running quickly.

---

## App Structure

The app uses a tab-based navigation with the following tabs:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Home** | Dashboard with today's summary and quick actions |
| **Daily Log** | Log what you eat each day |
| **Meal Plan** | Calendar — plan diets for specific dates |
| **Health** | Track weight, blood glucose, and other metrics |
| **More** | Access meals, diets, foods, grocery lists, settings |

---

## Recommended First Steps

1. **Add foods** — go to More → Foods and add the foods you eat regularly, or use online search.
2. **Build meals** — go to More → Meals and create your common meals.
3. **Create a diet** — go to More → Diets and build a full-day diet template.
4. **Plan your week** — go to Meal Plan and assign diets to each day.
5. **Log daily** — use the Daily Log tab to record what you actually ate.

---

## Data & Privacy

- All data is stored **locally on your device**.
- Authentication is handled by **Firebase** (Google's secure infrastructure).
- No meal or health data leaves your device.
- Cloud backup / cross-device sync is on the roadmap.
