# Food Database — iOS

The food database is the foundation of MealPlan+. All macros are stored per 100g and scaled when you specify quantities in meals.

---

## Adding a Food Manually

1. Go to **More → Foods → +**.
2. Enter the food name, calories, protein, carbs, and fat (per 100g).
3. Optionally add a default serving size, unit, brand, and category.
4. Tap **Save**.

---

## Online Search

1. Tap the **search icon** on the Foods screen.
2. Type a food name to query the USDA and OpenFoodFacts databases.
3. Select a result to import it into your food database.

---

## Editing & Deleting Foods

Tap any food → tap **Edit** to modify. Tap **Delete** to remove (only possible if the food is not used in any meal).

---

## Supported Units

`g` · `ml` · `piece` · `cup` · `tbsp` · `tsp` · `slice` · `scoop`
