# Diets — iOS

A **diet** is a full-day meal template. Assign meals to slots (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, etc.) and reuse the template across calendar days.

---

## Creating a Diet

1. Go to **More → Diets → +**.
2. Enter a name and optional description.
3. Tap each meal slot to assign a meal from your library.
4. Save.

---

## Diet Detail

Shows all assigned meals with per-slot and total daily nutrition. Tap a meal to view its ingredients with an interactive checklist.

---

## Tags

Tag diets with coloured labels (e.g. Remission, Maintenance, High Protein) to categorise and filter them.

---

## Assigning to a Date

Once created, assign a diet to any date via the **Meal Plan** calendar tab.
