# Daily Logging — iOS

Log everything you eat each day across meal slots. Compare actual intake against your planned diet.

---

## Opening the Log

Tap the **Daily Log** tab. Navigate between dates by tapping the date header or swiping.

---

## Logging Food

1. Tap **+** on a meal slot.
2. Choose **Select Meal** to add a full meal, or **Add Food** for a single item.
3. Set quantity and unit → tap **Add**.

---

## Applying a Diet

Tap **Apply Diet** at the top of the log to pre-fill all slots from a diet template in one tap.

---

## Custom Slots

Tap **Add Custom Slot** to create a one-off slot for that day (e.g. "Post-workout shake").

---

## Daily Summary

The summary bar at the top shows total calories, protein, carbs, and fat consumed vs your goal for the day.
