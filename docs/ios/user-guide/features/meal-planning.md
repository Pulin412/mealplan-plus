# Meal Planning — iOS

The **Meal Plan** tab shows a calendar. Plan ahead by assigning diet templates to specific dates.

---

## Assigning a Diet

1. Tap a date on the calendar.
2. Tap **Assign Diet**.
3. Select a diet template.

Planned dates are highlighted on the calendar.

---

## Changing or Removing

Tap the date → tap the assigned diet → choose **Change** or **Remove**.

---

## Viewing the Plan

Tap a planned date → tap the diet name to see all meals and their nutrition in read-only mode.

---

## Quick Log

Tap a date → **Log Meals** to jump to the daily log for that date, with the diet pre-applied.
