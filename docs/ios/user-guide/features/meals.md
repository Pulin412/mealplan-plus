# Meals — iOS

A **meal** is a named collection of foods with quantities. Create a meal once, reuse it in any diet or log.

---

## Creating a Meal

1. Go to **More → Meals → +**.
2. Enter a meal name.
3. Tap **Add Food**, pick a food, and set quantity and unit.
4. Repeat for all foods in the meal.
5. Tap **Save**.

---

## Meal Detail

Tap any meal to see:
- A checklist of all food items (tap to check off as you prepare).
- Total calories, protein, carbs, and fat.

---

## Editing & Deleting

Tap a meal → tap **Edit** to modify foods or rename. Tap **Delete** to remove (only if not used in any diet).
