# Getting Started — Android

## What is MealPlan+?

MealPlan+ is a meal planning and nutrition tracking app designed for people managing diabetes or following specific dietary plans. It lets you build a personal food database, create reusable meal and diet templates, plan your week on a calendar, log what you eat, and track health metrics like blood glucose and weight.

---

## Installation

1. Install the APK shared with you, or download from the Play Store (when available).
2. If installing from an APK, enable **Install from unknown sources** in your device settings.
3. Open the app — you will land on the sign-in screen.

---

## Creating an Account

You have two options:

### Option A — Sign in with Google
Tap **Continue with Google** and select your Google account. No password needed.

### Option B — Email & Password
1. Tap **Create an account**.
2. Enter your name, email address, and a password (minimum 6 characters).
3. Tap **Sign Up**.

> Your password is managed securely by Firebase — MealPlan+ never stores your plain-text password. You can reset it at any time using **Forgot Password** on the login screen.

---

## First Launch

After signing in for the first time, the app seeds your account with:
- A starter set of **common foods** (vegetables, grains, proteins, dairy)
- Sample **meal slots** (Breakfast, Morning Snack, Lunch, Evening Snack, Dinner)
- Example **diet templates** to get you started

You can delete or modify any of these.

---

## App Structure

The app has six main tabs at the bottom:

| Tab | What it does |
|-----|-------------|
| **Home** | Dashboard — today's plan, quick actions, summary |
| **Meal Plan** | Calendar view — plan diets for specific dates |
| **Log** | Daily food log — record what you actually ate |
| **Diets** | Your diet templates — reusable meal plans |
| **Health** | Log health metrics — weight, blood glucose, etc. |
| **Grocery** | Shopping lists — auto-generated from your plans |

---

## Recommended First Steps

1. **Add your foods** — Go to Home → Foods and add the foods you commonly eat, or scan barcodes.
2. **Build a meal** — Go to Home → Meals and create your first meal (e.g. "Breakfast Bowl") by combining foods.
3. **Create a diet** — Go to Diets → + and build a daily diet template by assigning meals to slots.
4. **Plan your week** — Go to Meal Plan and assign your diet to a day.
5. **Log your food** — Go to Log and record what you ate today.

---

## Profile Setup

Tap the profile icon on the Home screen to set:
- Display name and bio
- Age, weight, height
- Activity level and dietary goal
- Contact information

This information helps personalize your calorie and macro targets.

---

## Data & Privacy

- All your data is stored **locally on your device**.
- Your account credentials are managed by **Firebase Authentication** (Google's infrastructure).
- No meal data, health metrics, or personal information is sent to any server.
- If you clear the app data or uninstall, your local data will be lost. Cloud backup is planned for a future release.
