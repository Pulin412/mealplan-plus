# Daily Logging

The **Daily Log** is where you record what you actually ate on a given day. It compares your planned diet against actual intake and calculates your nutrition totals for the day.

---

## Opening the Log

- Tap the **Log** tab in the bottom navigation.
- Or tap **Log Today** on the Home screen.
- Or tap any date on the Calendar and tap **View Log**.

The log defaults to today. Swipe left/right or tap the date header to navigate to other days.

---

## Log Structure

The log is divided into **meal slots**:

| Slot | Icon |
|------|------|
| Breakfast | ☀️ |
| Morning Snack | 🍎 |
| Lunch | 🥗 |
| Evening Snack | 🌿 |
| Dinner | 🌙 |
| Custom Slots | ✏️ |

Each slot shows the foods logged in it and their calorie/macro contribution.

---

## Logging a Meal (from a template)

1. Tap **+** on a slot.
2. Choose **Select Meal** to pick from your saved meals.
3. Browse or search your meals — tap one to add all its foods to the slot.
4. Optionally adjust quantities before confirming.

---

## Logging an Individual Food

1. Tap **+** on a slot.
2. Choose **Add Food** to pick a single food from your database.
3. Set the quantity and unit.
4. Tap **Add**.

---

## Applying a Diet Plan

Instead of logging slot by slot, apply a diet template to pre-fill the entire day:

1. Tap **Apply Diet** at the top of the log screen.
2. Select a diet from your templates.
3. All meals from that diet are added to their respective slots.
4. You can then edit individual items as needed.

---

## Custom Meal Slots

For occasions not covered by the five standard slots (e.g. "Post-workout shake", "Pre-bed snack"):

1. Tap **Add Custom Slot** at the bottom of the log.
2. Enter a name.
3. The slot appears in the log for that day only.

---

## Daily Nutrition Summary

At the top of the log, a summary bar shows:
- **Calories** — total consumed vs goal
- **Protein** — grams consumed
- **Carbs** — grams consumed
- **Fat** — grams consumed

The goal values come from your assigned diet plan for that day (if any).

---

## Editing a Logged Item

Tap any logged food item to:
- Change the quantity or unit.
- Remove it from the log.

---

## Viewing Past Logs

Navigate to any past date using the Calendar tab or by swiping in the Log. All historical logs are preserved on device.

---

## Planned vs Actual

If you have a diet assigned to a date (via Meal Plan), the log shows both the planned meals and what you actually logged, so you can see where you deviated from the plan.
