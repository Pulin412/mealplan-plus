# Meal Planning (Calendar)

The **Meal Plan** tab gives you a calendar view of your diet assignments. Plan your week (or month) in advance by assigning diet templates to specific dates.

---

## Calendar View

The calendar shows the current month. Dates with a diet assigned are highlighted. Tap any date to:
- See which diet is planned for that day.
- Navigate to the daily log for that date.
- Assign or change the diet.

---

## Assigning a Diet to a Date

1. Go to the **Meal Plan** tab.
2. Tap the date you want to plan.
3. Tap **Assign Diet**.
4. Browse your diet templates and select one.
5. The diet is now planned for that date — its total calories and macros are shown on the calendar tile.

---

## Changing or Removing a Diet

1. Tap the date on the calendar.
2. Tap the currently assigned diet.
3. Choose **Change Diet** or **Remove**.

Removing a diet plan does not delete any logged foods for that date.

---

## Viewing a Planned Diet

Tap a date that has a diet assigned → tap the diet name to see its full meal breakdown (all slots and foods) in read-only mode.

---

## Quick Log from Calendar

Tap a date → tap **Log Meals** to jump directly to the daily log for that date. If a diet is already assigned, it is pre-filled in the log.

---

## Week at a Glance

The calendar highlights:
- **Today** — bold border
- **Planned days** — filled dot or diet name chip
- **Logged days** — distinct colour indicating data exists

This lets you quickly see gaps in your plan.

---

## Tips

- Plan the full week on Sunday — pick your diet templates for each day in advance.
- Use different diet templates for weekdays vs weekends.
- The Grocery List feature can generate a shopping list from your planned diets for a date range (see [Grocery Lists](grocery-lists.md)).
