# Grocery Lists

The **Grocery** tab lets you create shopping lists — either manually or automatically generated from your planned diets.

---

## Creating a Grocery List

### Auto-generate from a Meal Plan

1. Go to **Grocery** → tap **+**.
2. Choose **Generate from Plan**.
3. Select a **date range** (e.g. Monday to Sunday).
4. The app scans your diet assignments for that range and aggregates all required foods.
5. Quantities are summed across all meals — if "Brown Rice 50g" appears in three meals across the week, the list shows "Brown Rice — 150g".
6. Tap **Generate** to create the list.

### Create Manually

1. Go to **Grocery** → tap **+**.
2. Choose **Manual**.
3. Enter a list name.
4. Tap **Add Item** to add foods one by one with quantities.

---

## Managing a Grocery List

Open any list to see all items grouped by **category** (Vegetables, Grains, Dairy, Proteins, etc.):

- **Check off items** as you add them to your cart — checked items move to the bottom.
- **Tap an item** to edit the quantity or unit.
- **Swipe to delete** an item.
- **Progress bar** at the top shows how many items are checked.

---

## Categories

Foods are automatically grouped by their category when added to a list:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|---------|
| Vegetables | Broccoli, Spinach, Tomatoes |
| Fruits | Apple, Banana, Berries |
| Grains & Cereals | Rice, Oats, Bread |
| Proteins | Chicken, Eggs, Lentils |
| Dairy | Milk, Yogurt, Cheese |
| Fats & Oils | Olive oil, Nuts, Avocado |
| Beverages | Tea, Coffee |
| Other | Everything else |

---

## Deleting a List

Swipe the list left on the Grocery screen and tap **Delete**, or open the list → tap the **trash icon**.

---

## Tips

- Generate a new list every Sunday for the coming week.
- Check your pantry before generating — the list shows totals needed based on your meal plan, not what you already have.
- Use the category grouping to shop aisle by aisle efficiently.
