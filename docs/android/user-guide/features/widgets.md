# Home Screen Widgets

MealPlan+ provides three home screen widgets that give you at-a-glance information without opening the app. Widgets update automatically when your data changes.

---

## Available Widgets

### 1. Today's Plan Widget
Shows your planned meals for today, slot by slot. Tap any slot to jump directly to the daily log for that meal.

**Displays:**
- Diet name assigned to today
- Each meal slot and its assigned meal
- Calories for each slot
- A button to open today's log

### 2. Diet Summary Widget
Shows your macro progress for today — how many calories, protein, carbs, and fat you've consumed vs your target.

**Displays:**
- Calorie ring (consumed vs goal)
- Protein / Carbs / Fat totals
- Visual progress indicators

### 3. Mini Calendar Widget
A compact calendar that shows the current month. Dates with planned diets are highlighted. Tap a date to open the Meal Plan screen for that date.

---

## Adding a Widget

1. Long-press an empty area on your Android home screen.
2. Tap **Widgets**.
3. Scroll to find **MealPlan+**.
4. Long-press the widget you want and drag it to your home screen.
5. Resize if needed.

---

## Widget Settings

You can customise widget appearance:

1. Open MealPlan+ → go to **Settings → Widget Settings**.
2. Options include:
   - **Theme** — light, dark, or match system
   - **Show/hide macro details**
   - **Compact vs expanded layout**

Changes apply to all widgets on your home screen.

---

## Widget Deep Links

Tapping elements on a widget opens the relevant screen in the app:
- Tapping a meal slot → opens that slot in today's log
- Tapping a calendar date → opens the meal plan for that date
- Tapping a diet name → opens the diet detail screen

---

## Troubleshooting

**Widget shows stale data:**
The widget updates automatically when the app data changes. If it appears stuck, open the app once to trigger a refresh. The widget observes your Room database and updates reactively — no manual refresh needed.

**Widget is blank:**
This usually means no diet is assigned to today. Open the Meal Plan tab and assign a diet to today.
